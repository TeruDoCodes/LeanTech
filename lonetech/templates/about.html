About Our Store

We started in 2024 as a group of students with a shared passion for e-commerce and innovation.

We started in 2023 as a group of students with a shared passion for e-commerce and innovation. This project began as part of our coursework, but quickly turned into something we’re excited to present. We’ve learned a lot about building an online store from scratch, from product listings to user experience. Our goal is to showcase not just what we’ve learned, but also the potential of e-commerce to bring people together through accessible and easy-to-use platforms.

Though this is a class project, we’ve treated it as a real-world challenge, and we’re proud of what we’ve built. Thank you for checking out our work, and we hope it reflects our creativity, teamwork, and ambition to one day turn these skills into something bigger.
